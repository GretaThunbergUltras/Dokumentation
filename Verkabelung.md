---|---

## Ultraschallsensoren

Board | Sensor | Name
---|---|---
S0 | 1 | LEFT
S1 | 2 | LEFT45
S2 | 3 | LEFT_FRONT
S3 | 4 | RIGHT_FRONT
S4 | 5 | RIGHT45
S5 | 6 | RIGHT
S6 | 7 | BACK
